Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Who we are

This website is for The Old Blacksmiths, a holiday stay in Bridgnorth. Booking enquiries and guest communications are handled through Astley Property Group at info@astleypropertygroup.co.uk.

Information we collect

When you use the website, we may collect details you choose to send, such as your name, email address, selected stay dates, guest count, and booking enquiry message.

If you sign up for offers, we collect your name, email address, and confirmation that you agreed to receive promotional emails.

How we use your information

  • To reply to booking enquiries.
  • To manage date requests and guest communication.
  • To send occasional offers if you have signed up for marketing.
  • To keep a basic record of mailing list consent.

Marketing emails

We only send promotional emails where you have given consent. You can ask to be removed from the mailing list at any time by emailing info@astleypropertygroup.co.uk.

Calendar availability

The website may check connected Airbnb and Booking.com calendar feeds so guests can see unavailable dates. These feeds are used only to show availability and do not show private guest details on the website.

Cookies and local storage

This website uses essential browser storage to remember if you have closed the deals pop-up or accepted the privacy notice. It does not use advertising cookies in its current form.

Third-party websites

The website links to Airbnb, Booking.com, Instagram, and Facebook. Those services have their own privacy policies and are responsible for how they handle your information when you visit them.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact info@astleypropertygroup.co.uk.
