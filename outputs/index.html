A restored little retreat

Heritage character with a calm, modern finish.

The Old Blacksmiths blends oak doors, exposed brick, stone floors, soft textiles, and a handcrafted kitchen into a compact stay that feels warm as soon as you step inside.

There is an open-plan kitchen and lounge downstairs, a bright mezzanine sleeping space above, and thoughtful arrival touches including fresh flowers, a guest book, and a welcome basket.

Cook, settle, unwind

A proper cottage base for easy days away.

Breakfast bar seating, a gas hob, integrated storage, oak finishes, and a bright open-plan layout make the cottage simple to use for short breaks or longer Bridgnorth stays.

  • Kitchen island with three stools
  • Comfortable lounge with smart TV
  • Mezzanine bedroom with soft layered bedding
  • Fresh towels, guest essentials, and welcome touches
