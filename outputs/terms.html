Booking Terms

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Booking enquiries

Guests can choose available dates on the website and send an enquiry to info@astleypropertygroup.co.uk. A booking is not confirmed until the stay dates, price, payment terms, and any guest requirements have been agreed in writing.

Availability

The website checks connected Airbnb and Booking.com calendar feeds to help show unavailable dates. Calendar feeds can occasionally be delayed, so availability should be confirmed before travel plans are finalised.

Guest numbers

The Old Blacksmiths sleeps 4 guests. Please include the expected guest count when enquiring.

Payments

Direct booking payment details will be provided after your enquiry has been accepted. Do not send payment until you have received confirmation from the property team.

Changes and cancellations

Cancellation, amendment, deposit, and balance terms should be agreed before a booking is confirmed. Platform bookings made through Airbnb or Booking.com are subject to the terms shown on those platforms.

Guest care

Guests are expected to treat the cottage, contents, neighbours, and local area respectfully. Any damage, maintenance issue, or urgent concern should be reported as soon as possible.

Contact

For booking questions, email info@astleypropertygroup.co.uk.
